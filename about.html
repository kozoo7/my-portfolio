The Complete Path: From Design to Direction

The Foundation Years (2013-2015)

My professional journey began as a Junior Graphic Designer in the freelance world, where I learned the fundamental principles that would shape my entire career. During this formative period, I developed my foundational skills in design software and principles, working closely with senior designers who mentored me in the craft.

At El Reda Photography in Lebanon, I explored Photography and Videography, developing crucial skills in visual storytelling, composition, lighting, and narrative flow.

Key Skills Developed:

  • Foundational design principles
  • Visual composition and storytelling
  • Client communication
  • Photo and video editing techniques

Building Expertise (2015-2017)

At Al-Deraset in Lebanon, I progressed from Graphic Designer to Head of Graphic, marking a significant leap in my professional development. I innovated new printing methods, reducing costs while maintaining quality, and supervised the production of diverse print materials.

Key Skills Developed:

  • Leadership and team management
  • Strategic workflow optimization
  • Cost-effective design solutions
  • Print production expertise

Digital Transformation & Advanced Specialization (2017-Present)

This phase marked my embrace of digital design revolution. As a Social Media and UI Designer, I expanded into digital platforms and user interface design, creating compelling content and managing design teams.

From 2020, I specialized in VFX and CGI as a freelance artist, mastering industry-standard software like Adobe After Effects and Blender for international projects.

Key Skills Developed:

  • Social media content strategy and creation
  • Advanced VFX and 3D modeling
  • Comprehensive UI/UX design principles
  • International project management

Regional Expansion (2019-2021)

Working across the Middle East, including Mercati app in Qatar and Z-Playing Cards in Lebanon, I launched successful projects like "Cedars Playing Cards". At Jawaher Media in Qatar, I created over 200 social media assets, increasing engagement by 40%.

Key Skills Developed:

  • Cultural sensitivity in design
  • Large-scale content portfolio management
  • Data-driven design strategy
  • Brand identity development

Current Excellence (2023-Present)

As Art Director at Mediaology and Update Digital Marketing, I lead creative vision and execution for digital marketing campaigns while mentoring design teams and implementing innovative design strategies.

Skills Mastery:

  • Design Software: Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Blender
  • Creative Direction & Strategy
  • Project Management & Leadership
  • Digital Marketing & Content Strategy
